Enneagram

From Polytope Wiki The enneagram is a non-convex polygon with 9 sides. It is created by taking the first stellation of an enneagon. A regular enneagram has equal sides and equal angles. It is one of two regular 9-sided star polygons, with the other being the great enneagram. The name "enneagram" is often used to describe either of these two shapes.
